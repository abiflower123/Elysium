Frequently Asked Questions

What is Full Stack Development?

Full Stack Development refers to the development of both front-end (client-side) and back-end (server-side) parts of a web application. It involves working with databases, servers, APIs, and user interfaces.

What programming languages are covered in this course?

This course provides in-depth training in core programming languages like Java, Python, and C++, along with front-end technologies (HTML, CSS, JavaScript) and back-end frameworks (Node.js, Express.js).

Is placement assistance provided after the course?

Yes, Elysium Academy offers 100% placement assurance upon successful completion of the Full Stack Core Programmer training, including student job assistance, interview preparation, and resume building.

What is the duration of the Full Stack Core Programmer course?

The total duration of the course is 180 hours, split into 60 hours of theory and 120 hours of practical sessions, spanning approximately 180 days.

Are there any prerequisites for this course?

The course is designed for beginners, so there are no strict prerequisites. A basic understanding of computer operations would be beneficial, but not mandatory.
