Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need prior programming experience?

No prior programming experience is required. Our course starts with the fundamentals and gradually progresses to more advanced topics.

What is the course duration?

The complete course takes approximately 8 weeks to complete if you dedicate 10-15 hours per week. However, you can learn at your own pace.

Will I get a certificate?

Yes, upon successful completion of the course and final project, you'll receive a certificate that you can share with employers and on LinkedIn.

What support is available during the course?

You'll have access to our community forum, weekly Q&A sessions with instructors, and can email our support team with any questions.
